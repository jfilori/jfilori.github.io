1. Time of Day Based Greetings
Convers I: Ìkíni Láàrin Akin àti ìyá Rẹ̀
Greetings between Akin and His Mother
Akin: Ẹ káàárọ̀ o, màmá Good morning, Mummy
Mummy: Káàárọ̀, Akin
Ṣé dáadáa la jí?		 Good morning, Akin
Trust you woke up well?
Akin: A dúpẹ́ Thank you.
Convers II: Ìkíni láàrin ọ̀rẹ́ méjì, Ronkẹ́ àti Ṣadé.
Greetings between two friends, Ronke and Sade.
Ronkẹ́: Káàárọ̀ o Ṣadé. Good morning Ṣade.
Ṣadé: Káàárọ̀ o Rónkẹ́. Good morning Ronke
Ronkẹ́: Ṣé dáadáa ni? How are you?
Ṣadé: Dáadáa ni, a dúpẹ́ Fine, thank you.
Ronkẹ́: Ilé ńkọ́? Hope your family is fine?
Ṣadé: A dúpẹ́, àlàáfíà ni. We are fine, thank you.
Ronkẹ́: Káàárọ̀ o Ṣadé. Good morning Ṣade.
Ṣadé: Káàárọ̀ o Rónkẹ́. Good morning Ronke
Ronkẹ́: Ṣé dáadáa ni o? How are you?
Ṣadé: Dáadáa ni o, a dúpẹ́ Fine, thank you.
Ronkẹ́: Gbogbo Ilé ńkọ́ o? Hope your family is fine?
Ṣadé: A dúpẹ́ o, àlàáfíà la wà. We are fine, thank you.
Convers III: Ìkíni láàrin aládùúgbò
Greetings between neighbours
Aládùúgbò 1: Ẹ káàárọ̀ o. Good morning.
Aládùúgbò 2: Ooo! Ẹ káàárọ̀. Yes! Good morning
Aládùúgbò 1: Ṣé dáadáa la jí o? Trust you woke up well?
Aládùúgbò 2: A dúpẹ́ o Thank you.
Aládùúgbò 1: Àwọn ọmọ ńkọ́ o? How are your children?
Aládùúgbò 2: Àlàáfíà ni o. They are fine. Thank you.
Aládùúgbò 1: Ẹ káàárọ̀ o. Good morning
Aládùúgbò 2: Ooo! Ẹ káàárọ̀ o Good morning
Aládùúgbò 1: Ṣé dáadáa la jí o? Trust you woke up well?
Aládùúgbò 2: A dúpẹ́ o. Thank you.
Aládùúgbò 1: Àwọn ọmọ ńkọ́ o? How is your child?
Aládùúgbò 2: Àlàáfíà ni o. Ẹ ṣé. Fine, thanks.
Aládùúgbò 1: Ẹ káàárọ̀. Good morning.
Aládùúgbò 2: Ẹ káàárọ̀. Good morning
Aládùúgbò 1: Ṣé dáadáa la jí? Trust you woke up well?
Aládùúgbò 2: A dúpẹ́. Thank you.
Aládùúgbò 1: Àwọn ọmọ ńkọ́ o? How are your children?
Aládùúgbò 2: Dáadáa ni. Ẹ ṣé. Fine, thanks.
Convers IV: Ìkíni láàrin Olùkọ́ àti Akẹ́kọ̀ọ́
Greetings between Teacher and Pupils
(Àwọn akẹ́kọ̀ọ́ dìde dúró bí wọ́n ṣe rí olùkọ́.
The pupils stood when they saw the teacher.)
Akẹ́kọ̀ọ́: Ẹ káàsán, olùkọ́. Good afternoon, teacher.
Olùkọ́: Ẹ káàsán o, ẹ̀yin akẹ́kọ̀ọ́.
Ṣé dáadáa ni?		 Good afternoon, students.
Hope you are fine?
Akẹ́kọ̀ọ́: Dáadáa ni olùkọ́. We are fine, teacher.
Olùkọ́: Ẹ ṣeun o. Ẹ jókòó. Thank you. Have your seat.
Convers V: Ìkíni láàrin Délé àti Bàbá Rẹ̀
Greetings between Délé and His Father
(Délé dọ̀bálẹ̀ kí bàbá rẹ̀.
Délé prostrated to greet his father.)
Délé: Ẹ káàsán o, baba.. Good afternoon, father
Bàbá: Káàsán o, Délé.
Ṣé dáadáa ni?		 Good afternoon, Délé.
Hope you are fine?
Délé: Dáadáa ni, a dúpẹ́. Fine, thank you.
Convers VI: Ìkíni láàrin ọkùnrin méjì
Greetings between two men
(Ọkùnrin méjì pàdé ara wọn lẹ̀yin iṣẹ́.
Two men met on their way from work.)
Ọkùnrin 1: Ẹ kú ìrọ̀lẹ́ o. Good evening.
Ọkùnrin 2: Ooo! Yes!
Ọkùnrin 1: Ẹ kú àtàárọ̀.
Báwo ni iṣẹ́ lónìí? 		Trust your day is fine.
How is work?
Ọkùnrin 2: Dáadáa ni o, a dúpẹ́. Fine, thank you.
Convers VII: Ìkíni láàrin Bímpé àti Ìyá Rẹ̀
Greetings between Bímpé and Her Mother
(Bímpé kúnlẹ̀ kí ìyá rẹ̀.
Bímpé knelt to greet her mother.)
Bímpé: Ẹ káalẹ́ o, ìyá. Good evening, mother.
Ìyá: Káalẹ́ o, ọmọ mi.
Ṣé dáadáa ni? 		Good evening, my daughter.
Hope you are fine?
Bímpé: A dúpẹ́, mà. Thank you, ma.
2. Pleasantries
Convers I: Ìkíni láàrin Ọkọ àti Ìyàwó
Greetings between Husband and Wife.
(Ọkọ ń kí Ìyàwó rẹ̀ lẹ́yìn tí ó dé láti ibi-iṣẹ́.
A husband is greeting his wife on his arrival from work.)
Ọkọ: Ẹ kúulé o. Greetings to you.
Aya: Ẹ káàbọ̀ o.
Ẹ kú àtàárọ̀.		 Welcome.
Trust your day is fine.
Ọkọ: Ẹ ṣeun. Thank you.
Ọkọ: Ẹ kúulé o, aya mi. Greetings to you, my wife.
Aya: Ẹ káàbọ̀ o, ọkọ mi.
Ẹ kú àtàárọ̀.		 Welcome, my husband.
Trust your day is fine.
Ọkọ: Ẹ ṣeun, aya mi. Thank you.
Convers II: Ìkíni láàrin ọ̀rẹ́ méjì, Olú àti Kọ́lá.
Greetings between two friends, Olú and Kọ́lá.
(Olú àti Kọ́lá ń dágbére fún ara wọn lẹ́yìn tí wọ́n parí ìpàdé.
Olú and Kọ́lá bid themselves farewell after their meeting.)
Olú: Ó dàbọ̀ o, Kọ́lá. Good bye, Kọ́lá.
Kọ́lá: Ó dàbọ̀, Olú. Good bye, Olú.
Olú: Ká fire pàdé o. May we meet in peace.
Kọ́lá: Àṣẹ. Amen.
Olú: Ó dàbọ̀ o, Kọ́lá. Good bye, Kọ́lá.
Kọ́lá: Ó dàbọ̀, Olú. Good bye, Olú.
Olú: Ká fire pàdé o. May we meet in peace.
Kọ́lá: Àmín o. Amen.
Convers III: Ìkíni láàrin Jídé àti Bàbá rẹ̀.
Greetings between Jídé and his Father.
(Jídé ń dágbére fún Bàbá rẹ̀ nígbà tí ó fẹ́ lọ sùn lálẹ́.
Jídé greets his father when he was about going to bed.)
Jídé: Ó dàárọ̀ o, Baba. Good night, Father.
Bàbá: Ó dàárọ̀ o, ọmọ mi.
Àsùnjí o.		 Good night, my son.
May we wake safely
Jídé: Àṣẹ o, Baba. Amen, Father.
Jídé: Ó dàárọ̀ o, Baba. Good night, Father.
Bàbá: Ó dàárọ̀ o, ọmọ mi.
Ọ̀kọ̀ọ̀kan la ó jí o.		 Good night, my son.
May we wake one after the other.
Jídé: Àmín o, Baba. Amen, Father.
Jídé: Ó dàárọ̀ o, Baba. Good night, Father.
Bàbá: Ó dàárọ̀ o, ọmọ mi.
Oorun ire lá o sùn o.		 Good night, my son.
May we sleep in peace.
Jídé: Àṣẹ o, Baba. Amen, Father.
Convers IV: Ìkíni láàrin Ọmọ àti Ìyá rẹ̀
Greetings between a child and his mother
(Ọmọ wá á kí Ìyá rẹ̀ nílé, ó sì ń dágbére nígbà tí ó fẹ́ máa lọ.
A child came home to greet his mother and
was bidding her farewell when he was ready to leave.)
Ọmọ: Mo ti fẹ́ máa lọ o, Ìyá mi. Mother, I am about going.
Ìyá: Ó dáa. Ó dàbọ̀ o, ọmọ mi.
Láyọ̀ lo ó sọ̀ o.		 Okay. Goodbye, my child.
Safe Journey
Ọmọ: Àmín o. Ẹ ṣeun, Ìyá mi. Amen. Thank you, my mother.
Ọmọ: Mo ti fẹ́ máa lọ o, Ìyá mi. Mother, I am about to go.
Ìyá: Ó dáa, Ó dàbọ̀ o, ọmọ mi.
Láyọ̀ lo ó dé o.		 Okay, Goodbye, my daughter.
Safe journey.
Ọmọ: Àṣẹ o. Ẹ ṣeun, Ìyá mi. Amen. Thank you, Mother.
Convers V: Ìkíni láàrin ọ̀rẹ́ méjì.
Greetings between two friends.
(Ọkùnrin ń dágbére fún ọ̀rẹ́ rẹ̀ nígbà tí ó kí i tán nílé.
A man was bidding his friend farewell after paying
him a visit.)
Ọ̀rẹ́ 1: Ó dìgbà kan ná o, ọ̀rẹ́ mi. Goodbye, friend.
Ọ̀rẹ́ 2: Ó dáa, Ó dàbọ̀ o.
Láyọ̀ la ó pàdé o. 		Okay, Goodbye.
May we meet in peace.
Ọ̀rẹ́ 1: Àmín o. Amen.
Convers VI: Ìkíni láàrin ọ̀rẹ́ méjì.
Greetings between two friends.
(Obìnrin kan ń ̣pe ọ̀rẹ́ rẹ̀ lórí ẹ̀rọ-ìbánisọ̀rọ̀
láti kí i kú ọjọ́ mẹ́ta.
A woman is greeting her friend on phone.)
Ọ̀rẹ́ 1: Ẹ ǹlẹ́ o.
Báwo ni o?		 Hello.
How are you?
Ọ̀rẹ́ 2: Dáadáa ni o. Fine.
Ọ̀rẹ́ 1: Ṣé ẹ wà o?
Ṣé nǹkan ń lọ dáadáa o? 		Hope you are good?
Hope everything is fine?
Ọ̀rẹ́ 2: Mo wà o. A dúpẹ́. I am fine. Thank you.
Ọ̀rẹ́ 1: Mo ní kí n kí i yín ni o. I just wish to check on you.
Ọ̀rẹ́ 2: Aah! Ẹ ṣeun o.
Láyọ̀ lẹ ó délé o.		 Aah! Thank you.
May you get home safely.
Ọ̀rẹ́ 1: Àmín o. Amen
