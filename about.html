about myself

Fọlọrunṣọ Ilọri (PhD) is a consultant linguist with scholarly expertise in syntax (word and clause), semantics, contrastive and error analysis of Nigerian languages especially Yoruba, Igala, Itsekiri, Igbo, and Nigerian Pidgin (Naija) in relation to English. He consults on Yoruba (as source or receptor) language based translation (technical, religious, literary, etc.), subtitling, lexicography, and Yoruba as Foreign Language training for teachers and learners. He is also an expert media and music content provider and an indigenous languages and culture advocate.

JF Ilọri is a senior academic at the Department of Linguistics, African and Asian Studies, Faculty of Arts, University of Lagos, Akọka Yaba, Lagos, Nigeria.
