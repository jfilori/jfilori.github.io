Audio Exercise


A

Mo ní ajá.

I have a dog.

B

Bàtà dúdú

black shoe.

D

Ayọ̀ ń tẹ dùrù

Ayọ̀ is playing the piano.

E

Wo ejò yẹn!

Look at that snake!

Ẹ

Ẹyẹ ń fò.

A bird is flying!

F

Tolú ń fọn fèrè

Tolú is blowing the trumpet.

G

Ìyá wé gèlè.

Mother put on a head-tie.

GB

Gbágùúdá nìyí.

Mother put on a head-tie.

H

Ronaldo ń halẹ̀.

Ronaldo is threatening.

I

Iṣu yìí tóbi.

This yam is big

J

Jagunjagun Amẹ́ríkà

American soldier.

K

Kọ́kọ́rọ́ mi nìyí.

This is my key.

L

Labalábá bà lé òdòdó.

The butterflyperched on the flower.

M

Màlúù nìyí.

This is a cow

N

Ohun a ní là ń náání.

We cherish whatever we have.

O

Bọ́lá mu omi.

Bọ́lá drank water.

Ọ

Ọmọ ń rákòrò.

The baby is crawling.

P

Àwọn agbábọ́ọ̀lù tò sí orí pápá.

The players lined up on the field.

R

Ràkúnmí ńlá.

a big camel.

S

Ọ̀gbẹ́ni yìí ń mu sìgá.

This guy is smoking cigarette.

Ṣ

Obìnrin yìí wọ ṣòkòtò.

This lady wore a trouser

T

Tata méjì

two grasshoppers.

U

Ìrù ẹṣin

horse tail.

W

Wàrà sóyà nìyí

This is soya milk.

Y

Wo yànmùyánmú yìí!

Look at this mosquito!
